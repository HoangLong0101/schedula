# Schedula User Manual

Schedula helps spa, clinic, salon, nail, massage, and wellness businesses manage appointments, customers, staff, services, products, equipment, reminders, and business performance from one app.

This manual is written for everyday users. It explains what each screen is for and how to complete common tasks.

## 1. Who Should Use Schedula

Schedula supports three common roles:

- Owner: manages the business, staff, settings, customer data, bookings, and reports.
- Receptionist: creates and manages appointments and customer records.
- Staff member: views assigned appointments and updates appointment progress.

Your available screens and actions may depend on your account permission.

## 2. Main Navigation

After signing in, use the bottom navigation bar:

| Menu in app | English meaning | Use it to |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Trang chu | Home | View today overview, upcoming appointments, staff status, customer summary, and insights. |
| Lich hen | Appointments | Search, create, filter, check in, complete, cancel, and manage appointment payments. |
| Thong ke | Statistics | Review revenue, booking performance, busy hours, resources, staff, and customer return rates. |
| Tai khoan | Account | Manage business information, staff, customers, equipment, catalog, settings, subscription, and sign out. |
| + button | Add appointment | Quickly open the new appointment form from anywhere in the main app. |

The notification bell on the Home screen opens the notification center.

## 3. Create a New Business Account

Use this when your business is using Schedula for the first time.

1. Open the app.
2. Tap Register / Dang ky ngay on the login screen.
3. Complete the account step:
   - Full name
   - Email
   - Password
   - Confirm password
   - Accept the terms
4. Complete the business step:
   - Business name
   - Business type, such as spa, dermatology clinic, salon, nail, massage, dental clinic, yoga/fitness, or other
   - Short business description, if needed
5. Complete the contact step:
   - Address
   - Phone number
   - Website, if available
6. Complete the business hours step:
   - Weekday opening and closing time
   - Weekend opening and closing time
   - Use `HH:mm` format, for example `08:00` and `20:00`
7. Tap the final setup button.
8. Sign in with the new email and password.

New business accounts are created as Owner accounts.

## 4. Sign In

1. Open the app.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Tap Login / Dang nhap.

You can also use Google sign-in if it is enabled for your account.

Notes:

- Facebook login and Admin login may appear in the interface, but they are not fully available in the current app.
- If login fails, check that your email and password are correct and that your account has been added to the business.

## 5. Home Screen

The Home screen gives a quick daily overview.

Use it to see:

- Number of appointments today
- Number of new customers
- Upcoming appointments
- Staff availability
- Customer summary
- AI-style business suggestions
- Notifications

Common actions:

- Tap Add appointment / Them lich hen to go to appointments.
- Tap See more / Xem them near appointments to open the appointment list.
- Tap See more near staff to open staff management.
- Tap View all near customers to open customer management.
- Tap the notification bell to open notifications.
- Pull down to refresh the dashboard.

## 6. Create an Appointment

You can create an appointment from the center `+` button, from Home, or from the Appointments screen.

1. Tap the `+` button or Add appointment.
2. Choose the input mode:
   - Manual input: enter appointment details yourself.
   - AI automatic: take or choose an image of a message/note and let the app fill in details where possible.
3. Choose a service.
4. Select the appointment date.
5. Select the start time.
6. Select the end time.
7. Choose the staff member.
8. Enter the customer phone number or search value.
9. Enter the customer name.
10. Add notes if needed.
11. Tap Book now / Dat lich ngay.

The appointment is saved as confirmed.

Good practice:

- Always check the date, time, customer name, service, and staff before saving.
- If using AI image scanning, review every filled field before booking.
- Add notes for special requests, allergies, customer preferences, or preparation instructions.

## 7. Manage Appointments

Open Lich hen / Appointments from the bottom navigation.

You can:

- Search appointments by customer, staff, service, or ID.
- Filter by all appointments, today, or this week.
- Filter by staff member.
- View appointment counts and status summaries.
- See conflict warnings when staff or resource timing may overlap.

Each appointment card shows:

- Customer name
- Staff member
- Service
- Status
- Time and duration
- Date
- Payment status, when available

### Appointment Status Flow

Appointments usually move through this flow:

1. Pending
2. Confirmed
3. Checked in / In progress
4. Completed

Use the action button on an appointment card:

- Check-in: marks a confirmed appointment as in progress.
- Complete: marks an in-progress appointment as completed.

Important:

- If payment is not marked as paid, the app may prevent completion.
- Use the payment button before completing when required.

### Cancel an Appointment

1. Open Appointments.
2. Find the appointment.
3. Tap the delete/cancel icon.
4. The appointment changes to cancelled.

Cancel only when the customer or business no longer needs the appointment.

### Mark Payment Complete

1. Open the appointment card.
2. Tap the payment icon.
3. The app records the appointment as paid.

Payment status labels may include:

- Paid
- Waiting for payment
- Payment failed
- Payment cancelled

## 8. Notifications

Open notifications from the bell icon on the Home screen.

Notifications are grouped by:

- All
- Appointments
- Staff
- System

Use notifications to catch important issues, such as:

- A customer has not checked in.
- A staff member is unexpectedly absent.
- Reminder messages were sent.

You can open the related screen from a notification, such as Appointments or Staff.

## 9. Manage Customers

Open Account, then choose Customer Management, or tap the customer link on Home.

Use the Customer screen to:

- Search by customer name or phone number.
- Filter customers by status.
- Add new customer records.
- Edit customer records.
- Delete customer records.
- View visit count, last visit, future appointments, birthday reminders, allergies, and notes.

Customer statuses may include:

- Active
- New
- In treatment/recovery
- Needs follow-up

### Add a Customer

1. Open Customer Management.
2. Tap the `+` button.
3. Enter:
   - Full name
   - Phone number
   - Email, if available
   - Birthday, if available
   - Allergies or warnings
   - Health or service notes
4. Tap Add customer.

Customer name and phone number are required.

### Edit or Delete a Customer

1. Find the customer.
2. Tap Edit to change details.
3. Tap the delete icon to remove the customer.

Use customer notes carefully because staff may rely on them during service.

## 10. Manage Staff

Open Account, then choose Staff Management, or tap the staff link on Home.

Use the Staff screen to:

- View available, in-session, and absent staff counts.
- Add staff members.
- Edit staff details.
- Delete staff members.
- Set staff status.
- Set weekly shifts.
- Add specialties.

### Add a Staff Member

1. Open Staff Management.
2. Tap the `+` button or Add new staff.
3. Enter:
   - Full name
   - Role or specialty
   - Phone number
   - Email
   - Status: available, in session, or absent
   - Skills/specialties
   - Weekly shift for each day
   - Display color
4. Tap Add staff.

Staff name and role are required.

### Staff Shift Options

The weekly shift selector supports:

- Morning
- Afternoon
- Full day
- Off

Keep staff shifts updated so booking recommendations and schedules are easier to manage.

## 11. Manage Services and Products

Open Account, then choose Product and Service Catalog.

Use the Catalog screen to manage what your business sells and books.

The catalog has two tabs:

- Services
- Products

### Add a Service

1. Open the Services tab.
2. Tap Add new service.
3. Enter:
   - Service name
   - Price in VND
   - Duration in minutes
   - Category
   - Required resources, such as bed, massage machine, nail table, private room
4. Tap Save.

### Add a Product

1. Open the Products tab.
2. Tap Add new product.
3. Enter:
   - Product name
   - Price in VND
   - Unit, such as bottle, box, or item
   - Category
4. Tap Save.

### Edit or Delete Catalog Items

1. Open the correct tab.
2. Tap the edit icon on the item.
3. Change the details and save.
4. Tap the delete icon to remove an item.

Good practice:

- Keep service duration accurate so appointment times are easier to schedule.
- Keep service names clear so staff and receptionists choose the correct item.

## 12. Manage Equipment

Open Account, then choose Equipment Management.

Use Equipment Management to track devices, beds, rooms, and other resources.

You can:

- Search by equipment name or location.
- Add equipment.
- Edit equipment.
- Delete equipment.
- Mark equipment as in use.
- Mark equipment as available.
- View maintenance date and status.

### Add Equipment

1. Open Equipment Management.
2. Tap the `+` button.
3. Enter:
   - Equipment name
   - Location or room
   - Quantity
4. Tap Add equipment.

Equipment name and a valid quantity are required.

### Change Equipment Status

Use the action button on the equipment card:

- Mark as in use when the equipment is being used.
- Mark as available when it is free again.

Statuses may include:

- Available
- In use
- Maintenance

## 15. Sign Out

1. Open Account.
2. Scroll to Support.
3. Tap Sign out / Dang xuat.

Sign out when using a shared device.

## 16. Recommended Daily Workflow

At the start of the day:

1. Open Home.
2. Review today appointment count.
3. Check upcoming appointments.
4. Check staff availability.
5. Open Notifications and review urgent items.

When a customer books:

1. Tap the `+` button.
2. Add appointment details.
3. Confirm service, staff, date, and time.
4. Save the appointment.

When a customer arrives:

1. Open Appointments.
2. Find the appointment.
3. Tap Check-in.

When service is finished:

1. Mark payment as paid if needed.
2. Tap Complete.
3. Update customer notes if there is important follow-up information.

At the end of the day:

1. Review unfinished or cancelled appointments.
2. Check customer follow-up reminders.
3. Check equipment status.
4. Review Statistics for performance.

## 17. Tips for Clean Data

- Use the same phone number format for each customer.
- Do not create duplicate customer records if the customer already exists.
- Keep staff status and shifts current.
- Keep service duration and price updated.
- Add notes only when they help future service.
- Record allergies and warnings clearly.
- Mark payments before completing appointments.
- Review AI-filled booking details before saving.

## 18. Troubleshooting

### I cannot sign in

- Check your email and password.
- Ask the owner/admin to confirm your account exists.
- If using Google login, make sure your Google email is connected to the business.

### I do not see business data

- Sign out and sign in again.
- Make sure you are using the correct account.
- Ask the owner/admin to check your business access.

### I cannot complete an appointment

- Check whether payment has been marked as paid.
- Check whether the appointment has already been cancelled or completed.

### I cannot create an appointment

- Make sure service, date, time, staff, and customer name are filled in.
- Check whether the selected staff member is available.
- Review conflict warnings before saving.

### Statistics look wrong

- Pull down to refresh.
- Check that appointments have correct statuses.
- Check that completed services and payments are recorded.

### A feature opens but does not do anything

Some settings, payment, admin, Facebook login, and advanced AI options may appear in the interface before they are fully enabled. Use the core booking, customer, staff, catalog, equipment, notification, account, and statistics features for daily operations.

## 19. Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Appointment / Lich hen | A booked service for a customer at a specific time. |
| Customer / Khach hang | A person receiving services from the business. |
| Staff / Nhan vien | A team member who performs or manages services. |
| Service / Dich vu | A bookable treatment or appointment item. |
| Product / San pham | A sellable product item. |
| Equipment / Thiet bi | A resource such as a bed, machine, table, or room. |
| Tenant / Co so | The business account or location using Schedula. |
| Check-in | Marks that the customer has arrived or service has started. |
| Completed | Marks that the appointment is finished. |
| Cancelled | Marks that the appointment will not happen. |
| Follow-up / Tai kham | A customer who may need to return or be contacted again. |
